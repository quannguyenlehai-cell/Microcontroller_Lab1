This repository is used to submit Lab 1, Microprocessor-Microcontroler course include:
- Ex1: present the status of two LEDs are switched every 2 seconds.
- Ex2: present a cycle in this traffic light is 5 seconds for the RED, 2 seconds for the YELLOW and 3 seconds for the GREEN.
- Ex3: extend to the 4-way traffic light. Arrange 12 LEDs in a nice shape to simulate the behaviors of a traffic light.
- Ex4: add one 7 led segment to the schematic in Exercise 3.
- Ex5: integrate the 7SEG-LED to the 4 way traffic light.
- Ex6,7,8,9,10: simulate an analog clock, with 12 different number. The connections for 12 LEDs are supposed from PA4 to PA15 of the STM32.
